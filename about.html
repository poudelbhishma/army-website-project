About Us

This institute also known as AIM GURKHA Training center which was established unofficially on 2061 BS and was registered officially on 2071 BS by our respected chairman Mani Ram Rai. He alone took the risk and opened this training center. Through his long journey with this training center he faced many challenges but met a good helping hand whose name was Ganesh Rai also known as Nehang sir and they both stand strong like a mountain, faced many challenges and slowly took this institute at next level and now here we are.
Around 120-150 candidates comes every year to get trained in this institute.

Our Goals

Our main goal is to make our boys both mentally and physically strong, so that they can achieve what they are dreaming off. We train our boys by highly professional trainers with perfectly balanced equipments. We also teach them maths and English subject by professional teachers who are very fine at teaching. Our goal is to make sure none of our boys falls behind in this race of achieving their goals.

We make sure our boys feel safe and sound inside our premises. We also make sure about their entertainment so that, they feel fresh and energetic. Our goal is also to make a strong bonding between trainers, teachers and our candidates.
